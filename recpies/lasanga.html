A glorious lasagna recipe

Easy to make, delicious and filling; what more could one wish for in a dish? That's what lasagna is.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of lasagna plates
  • 800g ground beef
  • 2 onions
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 500g mashed tomatoes
  • 1l cream
  • 200g graded cheese

How to prepare

  1. Set oven to 200Â° C
  2. Cut onions and garlic
  3. Fry onion, garlic and ground beef
  4. Add mashed tomatoes
  5. Layer lasagna plates with meat-sauce
  6. Top up with cheese and cream
  7. Oven for 35 min
